About Me

I bring over two decades of experience in DeepTech and startup ecosystem

I am a co-founder, entrepreneur, and seasoned technology leader with deep expertise in both U.S. and Indian startup ecosystems.

As a strategic and technical advisor, I enable global startups across the USA, India, Europe, and Japan through collaborations with prestigious institutions like UC Berkeley Skydeck, Carnegie Mellon University's Schwartz Center for Entrepreneurship, UPenn Wharton's VentureLab, and leading Indian accelerators, including NASSCOM, T-Hub, and IIT-B eCell.

As a dedicated and seasoned startup enabler, I provide expert guidance in engineering, development, GTM, pitching, staffing, outsourcing, and strategic planning for early-stage ventures.

I serve as a fractional CxO and advisor specialising in software architecture, design, scalability, performance and quality assurance.

I am a strategic facilitator, enabler and builder of industry-academia partnerships, fostering collaborations between global startups and leading universities.

I am a highly experienced engineering professional in the wireless semiconductor industry, with nearly two decades of expertise at Qualcomm USA and Intel India.
